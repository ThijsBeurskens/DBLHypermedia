Introduction

The MSc Human-Technology Interaction is a two year program (120 ects). Apart from 25 ects mandatory courses, you choose minimum 25 ects of specialization electives in the domain of Human Technology Interaction and 15 ects of electives in a technical domain (engineering courses).

The second year mainly consists of the international semester (minimum 15 ects) and the graduation project (30 ects).

Osiris course guide.

Core Courses

    0HM100 Overview HTI (5 ects)

    0HM110 UX Design (Design Track A) (5 ects)

    0HM170 Research project (Design Track B + Research project) (10 ects)

    0HM120 Advanced Data Analysis (5 ects)

Specialization Electives

The specialization electives (25 EC) are separated into two categories:

  1. Choose at least 2 courses of B1 (10 EC)
  2. Choose at least 3 courses from B1 or B2 (15 EC)

B1 Choose >= 2 from below

    Advanced Cognition 0HM130

    Advanced Perception 0HM140

Engineering courses

Within the HTI master program, students are required to do at least 15 ETCS of engineering courses of their interest and expertise. These 15 ECTS can be done during the elective space in Q2, Q3, or Q4 of the first year, but you will also need to schedule some in Q1 and Q2 of your second year either during your international semester (of >=15 ects) or back at TU/e.
